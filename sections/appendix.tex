The no-NRTE branch has 6{,}652 feasible rows and the NRTE branch has 7{,}748 feasible rows out of the same 10{,}000 generated-row denominator. SPO and encircled-energy metrics are normalized by Airy diameter; nominal performance is left in its returned units.
