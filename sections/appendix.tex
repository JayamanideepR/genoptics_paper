\section{Dataset Details}
\label{app:dataset_details}

This appendix records the dataset construction details that are too dense for the main dataset section. The numbers in this section are computed from the canonical dataset artifacts used for the manuscript dataset variants.

\subsection{Canonical Splits}

Splits are assigned by patent parent family before creating the dataset-size variants. This prevents a patent seed and its synthetic children from appearing in different train/validation/test partitions. Table~\ref{tab:canonical_split_source_breakdown} reports the full canonical source counts before the 250k and 500k training subsets are drawn.

\subsection{Column Groups}

Table~\ref{tab:canonical_column_groups} summarizes the major column groups in the canonical table. Model-facing tuned and untuned representations are produced from this canonical table by separating prescription/design-condition variables from provenance, filtering, and optical-performance fields.

\begin{table}[htbp]
\centering
\footnotesize
\caption{\textbf{Canonical dataset column groups.}}
\label{tab:canonical_column_groups}
\resizebox{\linewidth}{!}{%
\begin{tabular}{ll}
\toprule
\textbf{Group} & \textbf{Examples and role} \\
\midrule
Surface geometry & \texttt{sXX\_00} curvature/radius fields, \texttt{sXX\_01} thickness fields, \texttt{sXX\_cir\_00} semi-diameter fields \\
Surface material & \texttt{sXX\_02} material labels; model-specific tuned representations convert labels to material-property variables \\
Topology & \texttt{surfcount}, \texttt{sto}, \texttt{isintstop}, active-surface structural masks \\
Design conditions & \texttt{efl}, \texttt{epd}, maximum field angle from \texttt{fldy\_04}, field/wavelength count columns \\
Field and wavelength specification & \texttt{fldx\_*}, \texttt{fldy\_*}, \texttt{xan\_*}, \texttt{yan\_*}, \texttt{wl00\_*}, \texttt{wtw00\_*} \\
Optical performance & \texttt{spo67\_*}, \texttt{spo100\_*}, \texttt{ee50\_*}, \texttt{ee90\_*}, \texttt{nomperf\_*} \\
Sensitivity and validity & \texttt{perfsens\_*}, \texttt{tolsens\_*}, \texttt{!error}, filter flags \\
Provenance and lineage & Source-folder, source-row, parent-patent, perturbation, sequence-file, and split columns \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
}
\end{table}
